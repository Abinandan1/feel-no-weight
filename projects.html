customers

commuters

Made for rugged travel allowing both hands to remain free to carry more things or to handle the unpredictable terrain of the wild, the backpack is the workhorse of the commuting world. Built for durability and balance, the backpack is useful if your commute happens to be through the woods, on a bike or underground in a train.

cyclists

Commuting by bicycle is fast becoming the best way for a lot of people to get to work. FNW bags offer features such as water bladders, helmet holders and are designed with specific features to make them more comfortable while bicycling.

trekkers

Your backpack is your companion throughout your trek. It’s either going to make your trek a pleasurable one or is going to be a burden on you. FNW has light frame backpacks for short & easy treks and durable backpacks for long & difficult treks.

runners

FNW offer lightweight bags to carry the water and food they need to fuel long runs, as well as some basic safety gear if they’re heading for the trails.
